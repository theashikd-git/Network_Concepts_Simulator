Networking fundamentals

IP AddressA logical address that identifies a device on a network and where to route data to.
MAC AddressA physical, hardware-burned address used to deliver frames on the local segment.
RouterConnects separate networks and forwards packets between them based on IP.
SwitchConnects devices on one local network and forwards frames using MAC addresses.
HubA dumb repeater broadcasts every signal to every port, no addressing logic.
GatewayThe router a device sends traffic to when the destination is outside its own network.
